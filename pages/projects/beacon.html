Beacon Dr

This project brought sophisticated updates to the powder bath, kitchen, and living room. The kitchen now features custom cabinetry and a unique natural quartz countertop with a peninsula waterfall edge. In the living room, fireplace inserts and the mantle were updated, complemented by new 5" wide white oak flooring that replaced previous wood to eliminate orange/red tones. The powder bath transformation included removing wallpaper and adding a Tahitian Pearl tile feature wall behind the vanity. The result is an elegant sparkle in every room, with a standout foyer light adding a unique touch not fully captured in photos.
