Lash Up Ln

This extensive project involved full transformations of three bathrooms, a basement entertainment area, and a walk-in closet. The bathrooms now showcase a range of custom tilework, from intricate herringbone patterns to natural pebble stone mosaics and a stunning floor-to-ceiling feature wall. A primary suite was reimagined into a waterfront sanctuary with a freestanding tub set against new, large-scale windows. In the basement, a sophisticated entertainment hub was created with a custom wet bar featuring dark wood cabinetry, quartz countertops, and a rustic wood accent wall. The walk-in closet was fully redesigned with a bright white organization system to maximize storage with tailored shelving and hanging space. The result is a collection of highly functional and beautifully appointed spaces, each elevated with unique, high-end finishes.
