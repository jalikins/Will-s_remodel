Wynward Road

Highlights from this recent project include a gourmet kitchen featuring a dramatic, full-height geometric tile backsplash and a large, two-tiered island for entertaining. An expansive, open-concept living area is defined by its dramatic wood grain vault ceilings and a floor-to-ceiling stacked stone fireplace with waterfront views. Finally, a bathroom was transformed into a spa-like retreat, showcasing a warm wood accent wall and a freestanding tub contrasted by modern, matte black fixtures.

The Living Room

Key features include dramatic wood grain vault ceilings, stacked stone fireplace, and updated layout designed to maximize the panoramic lake views.

The Kitchen

Key features include two-tone custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, two-tiered island, and full-height geometric tile backsplash. The new open-concept layout dramatically improved counter space for entertaining and daily prep work.

The Bathroom

A stunning wood accent wall and vanity set the tone for this spa-like bathroom. The design features a striking contrast between the warm wood and bold, matte black fixtures, a freestanding soaking tub, and a spacious walk-in shower.
